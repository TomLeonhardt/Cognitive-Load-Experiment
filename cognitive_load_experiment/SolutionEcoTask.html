Here is the solution to the task:

Determining the Nash Equilibrium:

Company A invests in advertising, Company B does not: (10, 1)

Company A does not invest in advertising, Company B invests: (1, 10)

If both companies choose the best response to the other company's decision, both will invest in advertising because it is their individually best response, regardless of the other company's decision.

Therefore, the Nash equilibrium is: Both companies invest in advertising: (4, 4).

Strategy Decision:

As the manager of Company A, you would choose to invest in advertising because it is the best response to the possible decision of Company B and leads to the Nash equilibrium.

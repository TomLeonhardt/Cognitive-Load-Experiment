Welcome to the "Cognitive Load Experiment"

On the next page, a sequence of numbers will be displayed.

Please memorize the sequence within the given time.

You will then need to keep this sequence in mind while working on a task.

After completing the task, you will be asked to recall and write down the sequence of numbers.

The experiment will consist of multiple rounds. In each round, you will memorize a new sequence of numbers and then complete an economics task.

Each round will be similar in structure, but the tasks may vary to test different aspects of cognitive load.

Note:

Remember, you are allowed to use AI during the tasks. You can choose to either use AI for assistance or solve the tasks independently.


You will have 20 seconds to memorize the numbers.
